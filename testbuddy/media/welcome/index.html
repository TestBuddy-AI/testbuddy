Welcome to TestBuddy


TestBuddy is here to help you automate your unit tests.

With TestBuddy you'll be able to generate test with a simple click from your editor! You can execute the tests on our test list view and get instant feedback of your code performance.

We know TestBuddy can sometimes be a bit silly with its responses, but not worry you can always ask him to modify the tests at your liking. Don't hesitate to push him with the hardwork.


Remember you can always use ctrl +alt + t / ⌘ (command) + ⌥ (option) + t
